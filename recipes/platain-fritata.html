Plantain Frittata Recipe

This is a simple recipe for plantain frittata.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe plantains
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of grated cheese (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Peel the plantains and cut them into thin slices.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and black pepper until well combined.
  4. Heat the vegetable oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium heat. Add the plantain slices and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they are golden brown.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the cooked plantains in the skillet. If using, sprinkle the grated cheese on top.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the frittata is set and lightly golden on top.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your plantain frittata!
